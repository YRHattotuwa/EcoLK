Friendly Soap by Eco Kadé

Friendly soaps are handmade using a range of natural,biodegradable ingredients offered by Eco Kadé. The soaps and shampoo bars are manufactured via the ancient cold process method which results in a rich, velvety lather and zero by-products. All their deliciously scented soaps come in recycled and recyclable card boxes. Friendly soap ensure that ethics and sustainability are given the top priority in their manufacturing process. The end result is a bar of soap that is free from nasties such as SLS and Parabens, as well as being vegan and cruelty free with plastic free packaging.

Available Soaps,

    Lavender
    Shea Butter
    Cocoa Butter
    Peppermint and Poppy Seed
    Aloe Vera

Bamboo Cups by Eco Kadé

Bamboo Cup are a range of reusable, biodegradable cups designed to preserve the taste and temperature of any drink offered by Eco Kadé. These colorful and vibrant cups are made from organic bamboo fibers along with corn flour and amino acid resins. The Bamboo Cup can be washed and reused for many years and the best part is once you’re done using it all you have to do is crumple the base, pour hot water over it and add it to an organic compost. The lid however is made from silicone which is not biodegradable but can be recycled.

Available Cup Designs,

    Donuts
    Guitar
    ZigZag
    Birds
    Flowers

Pens by Back To Earth

The pens by Back to Earth are made using throwaway Cinnamon Twigs and Tea Wood collected from the famed Ceylon Cinnamon and Tea plantations of Sri Lanka. Each individual product is skillfully handmade by crafts persons based in rural villages of Uva Province, Sri Lanka. These pens are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and can be recycled.

Available Pens,

    Tea Range Pens
    Cinnamon Range Pens

Compostable Bags by Eco Films
