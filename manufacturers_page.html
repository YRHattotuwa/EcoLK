Our Partners

There are many Eco-Friendly product manufacturers in Sri Lanka who export overseas. From them, we were able to partner up with few of the main manufacturers.


Eco Kadé is a small, eco conscious, ethical business that carefully sources affordable, well-crafted zero-waste products for Sri Lanka which is manged by the husband and wife duo, Munsif and Saara.
They were introduced to the concept of reusable, bamboo straws in Bali which inspired them to cut down on single use plastics ever since.
Through Eco Kadé, they aim to raise awareness about the impact of single use plastic on our health and ecosystems, and encourage us to embark on a zero-waste journey along them


Back to Earth is a family owned enterprise having begun as a small home-based workshop back in 1997, in the beautiful tropical island of Sri Lanka.
Their philosophy is to produce eco-friendly products that have the minimum impact on the environment. Therefore, they keep exploring new ways of making use of natural discards, up-cycled wood and recycled handmade paper.
Back To Earth have a skilled team working on the production of their wide range of handmade paper items while they also work with rural craftspersons who create the unique handmade wooden product range. (Can Remove this paragraph if you feel like there’s too much info.)
Back to Earth products make maximum use of discards from the famed Ceylon Tea and Ceylon Cinnamon Plantations of Sri Lanka, while also producing our own specialty range of handmade paper. They also collect discarded wood from factories and carpentries and up-cycle them into functional products.


Eco Films (Pvt) Ltd is a flexible packaging manufacturer based in Jaela, Sri Lanka , with subsidiary Eco Films Packaging based in Christchurch New Zealand. They specialize in manufacturing of comprehensive portfolio of printed and unprinted flexing packaging materials supplying a wide range of customers across all key market sectors in domestic and export. Their manufacturing facility is located in Jaela as well.
Eco Film is the holding company of Polytrad Industries and Evinco Polymers (Pvt) Ltd. Polytrad is a member of the Oxo-biodegradable plastic Association of United Kingdom (OPA). In Sri Lanka they are the sole distributor of Symphony Environmental products such as d2w, d2p and d2t from United Kingdom, Exclusive agents for Torise Bio- Materials, a world leading compostable Resin manufacturer in China and Agents for Calcium Carbonate Powder and Calcium Carbonate fillers.
